Frequently Asked Questions

Q. What is the full meaning of NASS

A. NASS means National Association of Science Students

Q. How many departments under NASS IBBUL

A. NASS IBBUL currently have eight(8) departments under its name and they are: 

            Biology
            BioChemistry
            Computer Science
            Chemistry
            Geography
            Mathematics
            Microbiology
            Physics

Q. How many students under NASS IBBUL?

A.NASS IBBUL have more than 5,000 students under its branch

Q. How do i become a member

A. Once yuo belong to any of the deoartments earlier mentioned above you're automatically a member of the association.

Q. How do i know the excos?

A. The excos can be easily known on this website by checking the our team page of this website. NASS IBBUL organises annual orientation program for all its members to enable each individual come to know more about the Association and IBBU lapai in genaral as new students find it difficult knowing their new environments quickly.

Q. Do i have to pay anything?

A. Yes, there is annual/tenure fees charge by the association in order to enable finances its activities and carry out its functions diligently.
