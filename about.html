About Us

National Association of Science Students was formed to bring together science students in all tertiary institution in nigeria. NASS IBBUL as a branch in of NASS in Ibrahim Badamasi Babangida University, Lapai serves as one of it umbrella body in the campus to unite all science students and give them one voice to speak or reach out and platform to collaborate as a team.

Since the existence of this association, it have prove to be of great help and importance in all nigerian tertiary institutions.
